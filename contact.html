Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on project scope, location, and deliverables. Contact us for a free, customized quote tailored to your specific needs.

What's your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days for edited footage. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you travel for projects?

Yes! We serve clients nationwide and are available for travel projects. Travel fees may apply depending on location.

What if weather is bad on shoot day?

We monitor weather closely and will reschedule if conditions are unsafe. Your safety and quality results are our priority.

Yes, we carry $2M in liability insurance and all our pilots are FAA Part 107 certified.
